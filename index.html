Keep the Hobbits out of Mordor!



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Eyes of Sauron, each in a different color, below. By clicking on an Eye, you will add a specfic amount of points to your total score.

To win (and keep the Hobbits out of Mordor), match your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number, and those filthy Hobbitses will complete their task to boot!

The value of each Eye is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each Eye!
